DJ Louie

Bio

Ayyyy! What’s good? This is DJ Louie here ready to bring you the bangers that your ears have been craving for. You can expect me to bring out the heavy hitting bass drops mixed in with a little liquid bass for the soul, and some pop EDM for the singalongs. I grew up right next to a festival in Michigan called Electric Forest, and this festival helped me discover my love for the genre of Electronic Dance Music. I fell in love with the memories that I made while listening to this music, and each song or artist that I listen to brings me back to a moment in time. My favorite part of this genre is the ability to “feel” the music’s bass rather than just listen. Also, there are always new upcoming artists, so there is something out there for everybody. This genre is on the rise, and each day it’s easier to talk about this genre of music with other people. So, grab a friend and vibe together to some of best music that EDM has to offer. Join me on the mothership and let’s explore :)
