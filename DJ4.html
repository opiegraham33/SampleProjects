Autobiography

Hey music lovers! I'm DJ Da-X from Holland, MI. I grew listening to all different types of music as a kid, and then in 5th grade, my bestfriend introduced me to a popular boy group named, Big Bang. Once I started listening their music, I got hooked on and since then I've always listened to K-Pop music.

The rise of Korean music first started to grow popular overseas in the mid 2000s era and started to grow more popular these days as one of the top music people listen to. K-Pop is more than just music, it's also about dancing and visualization.
